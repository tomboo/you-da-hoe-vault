071715 Betsy reveals Hazey loan and face lift

"A face lift? If I won the lottery, I'd go for a butt lift!" Margaret said. "Is there such a thing as butt-removal?"

Betsy, who was a nice person, tsk-tsked. "You look fine, Margie. Besides, you got two kids out of the deal. All I've got is my eyelids stapled on top of my head."

Both women laughed. Margaret remembered that Betsy had gotten divorced after marrying her college boy friend. It wasn't like eligible men---hell, men with health care---were thick on the ground in Coeur D'Alene. Small town living had its advantages but marriage after divorce wasn't one of them.
