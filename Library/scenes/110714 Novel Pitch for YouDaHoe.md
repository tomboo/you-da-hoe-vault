110714 You-Da-Hoe pitch

What if the crazy person was outside the nuthouse?

What if you were such a screw up, you kept screwing up your own suicide so your guardians dumped you in backwoods Idaho with a bunch of other screw-ups to help you get sane? In this literary exploration of the thin line between craziness and immaturity, five troubled adolescents battle the temptations of alcohol, drugs, sex and doing whatever the hell you want while locked up at Inner Horizons, a psychiatric treatment center in rural Idaho.

The only problem is that nobody seems to notice that the doctor who runs the Program is writing prescriptions for people who aren't there and a psychopath stalks the treatment center in a garbage truck.

In this literary exploration of the thin line between craziness and immaturity, six troubled souls battle their own impulses to do the right thing in the teeth of drugs, booze, sex and boring normalcy.

In this literary exploration of how to make the right choices in the teeth of drugs, booze, sex and boring normalcy, you'll meet the struggling souls of Inner Journeys. A Dilaudid-addled Head Doctor sufer who accidentally drove over his best friend, a sex-addicted heir to a catsup fortune, a housekeeper willing to do anything for health insurance and are among the characters faced with life's toughest choices: how to live a oral life in a world of temptation and chaos.

What if you kept trying to kill yourself and you kept screwing up? What if you were such a screw up, they locked you up with a lot of other screw-ups in backwoods Idaho in order to get sane? This is the situation Old Girl finds herself in when she's locked up with a group of troubled teens at Inner Journeys, a psychiatric treatment program outside Couer D'Alene, Idaho.

You-Da-Hoe is a literary exploration of how to make the right choices in the teeth of drugs, booze, sex and boring normalcy, you'll meet the struggling souls of Inner Journeys. A Dilaudid-addled Head Doctor, a surfer who accidentally killed his best friend, a sex-addicted heir to a catsup fortune, a housekeeper willing to do anything for health insurance and a girl with a suicide fixation are among the characters faced with life's toughest choices: how to live a moral life in a world of temptation.

In the world of You-Da-Hoe, everybody has to walk the thin line between craziness and sanity on the journey to maturity.
