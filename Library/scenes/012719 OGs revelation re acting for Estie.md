012719 OGs revelation re acting for Estie

OG thought for a minute or two that she was acting on Estie's behalf, like she had when she pushed the platter of food back in front of the girl. But feeling Dani struggle and flail under the hand that held her down, OG realized something interesting. It wasn't Estie she was acting for, it was herself. And man, did it feel good to push Dani's face up to her eyebrows in the pan.
